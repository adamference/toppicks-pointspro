Summary of Data Practices

While the full privacy policy is maintained on PointsPro.org, here is a brief overview of what data we collect and how it's used on this site:

Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to remember your preferences, understand how you use our site, and improve your experience. These include first-party cookies set by our site and third-party cookies set by our analytics and advertising partners.

Analytics

We use Ezoic's analytics platform to understand site traffic, page performance, and user behavior in aggregate. This helps us improve our content and user experience. Analytics data is collected in a way that does not personally identify individual users.

Ad Personalization

This site uses Ezoic for ad serving and optimization. Ezoic and its advertising partners may use cookies and similar technologies to serve personalized advertisements based on your browsing history and interests. You can manage your ad preferences and consent through the cookie consent banner displayed on this site.

Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect your credit score, Social Security number, financial account information, or any other sensitive personal data. Our card finder quiz runs entirely in your browser — no quiz data is transmitted to our servers. When you click through to apply for a card, you leave our site and interact directly with the card issuer.
