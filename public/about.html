About TopPicks PointsPro

Helping consumers find the right credit card since 2024.

Who We Are

TopPicks PointsPro is a credit card comparison and education platform operated by PointsPro.org, a property of Northern Light Publishing. Our mission is to simplify the credit card selection process by providing clear, unbiased information that helps consumers make informed financial decisions.

We believe that everyone deserves access to straightforward guidance when choosing a credit card — whether you're building credit for the first time, rebuilding after a setback, or optimizing rewards on everyday spending. Our team researches and evaluates card offers across dozens of issuers to surface the options most relevant to your credit profile and goals.

Editorial Independence

Our editorial content is produced independently from our business relationships. While we maintain partnerships with card issuers, these relationships never dictate our ratings, reviews, or recommendations. Our editorial team evaluates every card using the same objective criteria regardless of whether we have a commercial relationship with the issuer.

How We Make Money

TopPicks PointsPro earns revenue through affiliate commissions. When you click on a card offer on our site and are approved by the issuer, we may receive compensation from that issuer. This compensation helps us keep our service free for consumers. It may influence which products we write about and where they appear on our pages, but it does not affect our editorial ratings or the honesty of our reviews.

Our Commitment

We are committed to accuracy, transparency, and putting the consumer first. Card terms, rates, and offers change frequently, and we work to keep our information as current as possible. If you ever find outdated information on our site, please let us know so we can correct it promptly.
