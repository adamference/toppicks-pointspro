How We Rate Credit Cards

Our editorial team evaluates each card across five dimensions: reward value, fee structure, approval accessibility, cardholder benefits, and customer satisfaction. Each card receives a score from 1 to 5 stars based on a weighted average of these factors.

We update our rankings monthly to reflect changing offers, new card launches, and shifts in market conditions. When issuers change terms or introduce limited-time promotions, we adjust our ratings accordingly.
